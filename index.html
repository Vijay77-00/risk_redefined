Frequently Asked Questions

Are you an insurance company or a financial advisory platform?

We are a trusted financial solutions partner, not a single insurance company. We partner with India's leading, IRDAI-regulated insurance providers and asset management companies to bring you a curated selection of life insurance, health insurance, mutual funds, and loan products under one roof — giving you completely unbiased, tailored recommendations based on your specific goals.

How do you choose the right financial or insurance plan for me?

We don't believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. We start with an Initial Consultation to understand your financial standing, milestones, and family needs. Using these insights, our experts analyze the market to build a Custom Strategy mapped precisely to your life goals, ensuring you only pay for the coverage and investments you actually need.

What is the difference between Term Life Insurance and Permanent/Endowment plans?

Term Life Insurance is pure, affordable protection that pays a guaranteed lump sum to your family if something happens to you during the policy term — ideal for high-cover needs like protecting a home loan or your child's education. Permanent or Endowment plans combine life cover with a savings or investment component, building cash value over time that you can access later in life.

How much health insurance coverage do I actually need?

While a basic corporate health policy is a good start, it is rarely enough for major emergencies or rising healthcare costs. We generally recommend a base cover or a Super Top-up plan that accounts for your family size, medical history, and city of residence. During our discovery call, we can calculate the exact buffer you need to protect your savings.

Why invest in Mutual Funds through a platform like yours instead of doing it myself?

Navigating thousands of schemes, market volatility, and asset allocation can be overwhelming. We provide expert portfolio management, helping you balance risk and reward based on your benchmarks, plus Continuous Reviews to dynamically rebalance your investments as the market shifts or your life goals change.

Are there any hidden fees or charges?

Absolutely not. We pride ourselves on Absolute Transparency — no hidden fees, structural surprises, or onboarding charges. All premium costs, fund management fees, and policy terms are disclosed upfront before implementation, so you can make an informed decision with total peace of mind.
