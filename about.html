About Us

Looking at the bigger picture of your financial security.

At Risk Redefined Insurance Marketing LLP, we believe that true financial security comes from looking at the bigger picture. Established in 2017 in Bangalore, we are a premier risk advisory and wealth distribution firm trusted by an esteemed clientele to safeguard their futures and optimize their assets.

We act as a comprehensive, single-window destination for complex financial needs. By bridging the gap between our clients and India's most reputable institutions — including leading Banks, Insurance Companies, Mutual Fund AMCs, and NBFCs — we dismantle the one-size-fits-all approach. Our success is measured exclusively through one metric: building customized strategies that deliver long-term client satisfaction and absolute financial clarity.

Our Leadership Team

Satish Kulkarni

Founder & Principal Officer

58 years old professional with over 37 years of experience in Banking, Mortgages, Insurance and Real Estate field while working for ICICI Bank, DHFL group, Brigade group, SEPL group and Risk Redefined Insurance Marketing LLP. His last assignment with ICICI Bank was that of a Deputy General Manager, heading Real Estate Valuations for the Bank while earlier, he handled various Regional /Zonal and National responsibilities in Mortgages and Property Services divisions. The last assignment in DHFL group was Business Head for DHFL Property Services while earlier he handled various responsibilities in Regional and Zonal levels in Mortgages. His previous two assignments before promoting Risk Redefined Insurance Marketing LLP were those of Chief Operating Officer in Tandem Allied Services Pvt Ltd ( a Brigade Group Company) and Managing Director of Shejawadkar Design and Constructions Pvt Ltd.

C V Gorabal

Managing Partner

An astute financial ecosystem professional, Gorabal brings 37+ years of deep operational mastery across Mortgages, Insurance, and Mutual Funds. Before co-promoting Risk Redefined, he played pivotal roles at India's top NBFCs and financial powerhouses including DHFL, Weizmann Homes (now L&T Finance), Gruh Finance (now Bandhan Bank), and PNB MetLife India Insurance — spanning Branch Operations, P&L Management, Internal Audits, and Vigilance. He is passionate about wealth creation and guides clients through custom market-linked strategies.

Anuradha Kulkarni
